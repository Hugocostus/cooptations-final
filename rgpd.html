Data Protection Policy (GDPR)

In accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), this page details how the data collected on this portal is processed by the Student Corporation Council.

1. Data Controller

Data processing is carried out by the emlyon Students Corporation Council, responsible for managing the cooptation processes.

2. Data Collected

  • Identity: First Name, Last Name.
  • Identifiers: Student ID number.
  • Contact: Email address (student or association).
  • Choices: Association wishes and cooptation lists.
  • Timestamps: Date and time of form submissions.

3. Purposes of Processing

  • Registration Management: Recording users in the system.
  • Authentication: Securing access to personal areas.
  • Collection of Wishes: Centralizing preferences for the cooptation process.
  • Communication: Sending confirmations and summaries via email.

4. Retention and Security

Data is kept for the duration of the current year's cooptation process and archived for a period of 2 years. It is transmitted via secure protocols and stored in databases with restricted access.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, rectify, and delete your personal data.

To exercise these rights, please contact the Data Protection Officer:

Email: cooptations@corpo-emlyon.com
