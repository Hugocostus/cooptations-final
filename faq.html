Frequently Asked Questions

For Students

I can't log in, what should I do?

Ensure you are using the email address and student ID provided during registration. If the issue persists, contact the administration as your profile may not be validated in the database yet.

Can I modify my choices after clicking "Submit"?

The system records every submission with a timestamp. In case of multiple submissions, the latest form submitted is generally the one taken into account by administrators.

Why are some associations missing from the list?

The list of available associations is predefined. If an association is missing, it may not be participating in the cooptation process through this portal this year.

For Associations

How do I get the access code for the Association page?

The code is sent exclusively to the association representative by the Students Corporation Council. Without this code, access is blocked and you will be redirected to the homepage.

I can't find a student in the search bar, why?

The dynamic search only works for students already registered via the registration.html page. If a student cannot be found, please ask them to complete their registration first.

How many names can I submit?

The table is designed to hold up to 150 names per session. Remember to specify the exact number of authorized coopted members in the dedicated field before validating the list.

Security and Data

Who can see my choices?

Your choices are confidential. They are only visible to system administrators to ensure the fairness of the process.

Right to be forgotten (GDPR)

You can exercise your right to have your data deleted by writing to cooptations@corpo-emlyon.com.
